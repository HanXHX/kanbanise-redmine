Kanbanise

Display a Redmine issues list in a Kanban format

How to use

Simply drag the link below to your bookmarks bar

kanbanise v0.4

Then, go to a page of Redmine issues. Filter it how you want, then click the "kanbanise" button in your bookmarks toolbar and you'll see your Redmine issues in Kanban format! Simply click to dismiss.

It doesn't do anything "smart". Like Ron Burgandy, it will simply read whatever is on the teleprompter (screen) so do all your filtering up front in Redmine!
